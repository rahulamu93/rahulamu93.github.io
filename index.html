About me

I am a PhD researcher at IIT Jodhpur specializing in Control & Power Electronics, with expertise in motor control, embedded systems, and hardware design. My research focuses on developing advanced sensorless control strategies for electric vehicle drivetrains and industrial motor drives.

With a strong foundation in electrical engineering from AMU Aligarh and industry experience as a Senior Motor Control Engineer at Reakar Electric India, I bridge the gap between academic research and real-world applications in power electronics and motor control systems.
